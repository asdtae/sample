About Sample

Sample is an innovative physics project that transforms radio signals into live video feeds using microcontroller technology. By combining three radio transmitters, a receiver, and advanced algorithms, we turn complex scientific principles into a captivating and interactive experience.

Our Goals

01

Make science accessible and engaging for all audiences.

02

Showcase the potential of radio signal analysis and microcontroller technology.

03

Provide a hands-on learning experience that inspires curiosity and discovery.

Meet the awesome team behind Sample. We’re bridging the gap between the world’s oldest and largest industry and a future where technology can be used to solve our hardest problems.
